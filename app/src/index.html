OSMOSCAPE

Echoes of the Osmotic Landscape by Nandita Kumar

 

About:

Osmoscape is a collaborative effort and a culmination of three years of research, which seeks to explore a new way of archiving data, and sound that can be intuitively understood through a sound and data based interactions. The idea is to have an interactive book which is also accessible through a web app that is free and open source.

 

Demos and code:

We have few demos for you to try: https://app.osmoscape.com/examples

What you will see once you open the link below is a sound composition scroll, which is designed as a long landscape. “Navigation”, “Legends & popup” should give you a fair bit of idea on the interaction for scroll. It’s currently work in progress, so if you find any bugs, great! Just let us know and if possible any solutions too. 

You can find all the source code here: https://github.com/TimeblurStudio/Osmoscape

Note: preferred browser is chrome, device can be laptop/desktop
